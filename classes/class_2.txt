Class: Parking Lot

Attributes:
- available_parking_spaces (integer)
- lot_full (boolean)
- staff_parking (array)
- towing_company (string)

Methods:
- add_cars (decreases available_parking_spaces integer)
- close_lot (sets lot_full boolean to true)
- add_staff_parking (adds additional parking designated to staff)
- update_towing_company (sets towing_company string to new company)
